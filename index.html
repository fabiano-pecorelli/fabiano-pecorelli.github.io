About Me

His primary research interests span a range of topics, including software maintenance and evolution, software testing, empirical software engineering, social software engineering, AI and machine learning engineering, and quantum software engineering. He has authored over 40 scientific articles published in international journals and conferences.

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Science and Information Technologies at Pegaso University, Italy. Before, I have been a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Software Engineering Salerno (SeSa) Research Lab at the University of Salerno, Italy, the Jheronimus Academy of Data Engineering (JADE) lab at Jheronimus Academy of Data Science (JADS), Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands, and the Cloud Software Evolution and Assessment (CloudSEA) research group at Tampere University, Finland. He received his Bachelor's (2016, cum laude), Master's (2018, cum laude), and Ph.D. (2022, cum laude) in Computer Science from the University of Salerno, Italy.



Myprimary research interests span a range of topics, including Software Maintenance and Evolution, Software Testing, Empirical Software Engineering, Social Software Engineering, AI and Machine Learning Engineering, and Quantum Software Engineering. I'm actively serving as a referee for various international journals in the field of Software Engineering (e.g., TSE, TOSEM, EMSE, JSS).
